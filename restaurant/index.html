Our Story

A Legacy of Flavour
Since 1987

Nestled in the heart of Paris, La Belle Assiette has been a sanctuary of French gastronomy for over three decades. Founded by Chef Élodie Marchand, our kitchen celebrates the finest local produce, elevating tradition with a modern and artful touch.

Every plate that leaves our kitchen is a labour of love — from the hand-rolled pasta to the house-made sauces simmered for hours. We believe that exceptional food begins with exceptional ingredients, sourced daily from trusted local farmers and producers.
